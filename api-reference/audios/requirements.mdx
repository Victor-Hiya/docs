sidebarTitle: Requirements

To upload a media file as an [audio][1] it must meet
the following requirements.

## Duration

The duration of the media file must not exceed 5 minutes.

## Voice activity

The platform must detect at least 1 second of speech.

## Format and codec

The format and codec of the media file must be
decodable by [FFmpeg][2].

You may click on the cards bellow to go to the
appropriate sections of their website, where
the supported formats and codecs are listed.
