Organizations allow collaborative management of their [spaces][2]
by the [users][1] who are members.

<Tip>
  To learn how to review the [usage records][3] of an organization,
  check the usage section.
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Access:** To manage an organization, you must be one of its members.
</Note>
